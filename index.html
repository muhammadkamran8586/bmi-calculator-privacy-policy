Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 04, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the BMI Calculator mobile application. Your privacy is important to us, and this document explains how we handle information.

1. No Data Collection

The BMI Calculator app is designed as a utility tool. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or health information. All calculations (Height, Weight, Age) are performed in real-time on your device and are never saved or uploaded to any server.

2. No Local Storage

The app does not use any local database or persistent storage to keep track of your BMI history or personal metrics. Once the app is closed, all session data is cleared.

3. Permissions

The app does not require any sensitive permissions (like Camera, Location, or Contacts) to function.

4. Third-Party Ads

If the app displays advertisements, those service providers (e.g., Google AdMob) may use unique identifiers on your device to show relevant ads. We do not have access to that data.

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
